CHIH-CHEN-HSING Tech.Co.Ltd is a manufacturer dedicated to metal finishing technology over a couple of decades. We focus on electrodeposition and sand-blasting and provide manufacturing technology advisory. We advisory services and work with our clients to solve their problems. All of our paint, producting facilities and are qualified.

Electrodeposition

Electrodeposition paint is high liquidity compared to other finishing technology and is more capable to avoid flow marks. With it, the paint smoothly covers workpieces and including the undercut surface and creates a corrosion protection cover. Metal workpieces like aluminum、magnesium、zinc、iron and synthetic metals work well withcthe electrodeposition process.

Sand Blasting

Sandblasting physically adjusts the surface of the workpieces. Through this process, we can increase the adhesion or polish the surface so as to make it smoother, shinier, attachable, and so on.
