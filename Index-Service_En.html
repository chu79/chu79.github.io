Electrodeposition

"ED Coating" Electrodeposition paint is high liquidity compared to other finishing technology and is more capable to avoid flow marks. With it, the paint smoothly covers workpieces and even the undercut surface and creates a corrosion protection cover. Electrodeposition is practical for higher accuracy demands, such as automotive components, machine components, screws, bolts, and so on.
