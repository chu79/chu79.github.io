CHIH-CHEN-HSING Tech.Co.Ltd

Locating in Nangang Industrial Park, we dedicate to metal finishing technology for over three decades, and we mainly focus on “Electrodeposition” and “Sandblasting”. Recently, we relocated our company to a wider space and upgraded our facilities to expand the production. All our facilities are certificated and proved by the environmental protection bureau and also the paint passes the RoHS testing as we do really care about environmental sustainability.

We more focus on electrodeposition with the most popular black paint and supply a little amount of sandblasting orders. Compared to other finishing technologies, electrodeposition is high liquidity and is more capable to avoid flow marks. With it, the paint smoothly covers workpieces and including the undercut surface and creates a corrosion protection cover. The materials such as aluminum, zinc, magnesium, iron, and other alloy metal are appliable for electrodeposition.

We had cooperated with different companies producing from small to big size products such as hand tools, stationaries, furniture, electronic products, automotive components, gymnastic tools, and so on.

公司成立前

建宏企業社成立

在...後，董事長決定自己成立電鍍公司，而後於家鄉開設建宏企業社，從草創時期艱困日子逐漸發展10多人左右的小型工廠
